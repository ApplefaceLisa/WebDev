Organic and Gluten-free

We use organic and gluten-free wheat flour to make our Indian Uthappam(pancake) and Italian pizza.

Hand-tossed Pizza

All pizza are hand-tossed and baked by our executive chef Alberto Somayya.

Super Supreme

Always a house favourite, combining juicy ground beef, smoky cabanossi sausages, beef pepperoni, chicken ham, and a luscious mix of capsicums, onions, olives, mushrooms and pineapple chunks.

Curry Chicken

Italian classic meets Indian favourite. Chunky chicken, potatoes and diced tomatoes come together for an authentic flavour of rich spices.

Pepperoni

Combining the aromatic flavour of beef pepperoni and 100% mozzarella cheese, this is one classic treat that’s always a great choice.

Veggie Lover's

A delightful combination of garden fresh onions, olives, capsicums and mushrooms, sweetened with pineapple chunks and diced tomatoes.

Hawaiian

An all-time favourite. Tender chicken ham combined with sweet and succulent pineapple chunks for a taste of the good old days.
